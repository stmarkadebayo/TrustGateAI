# Oil and Gas Compliance Glossary

Local content refers to the quantum of value added to the Nigerian economy through the use of Nigerian human and material resources.

An OEM authorization letter is a formal document showing that a supplier or contractor is recognized by the original equipment manufacturer to represent, distribute, or support the relevant product line.

Registry validation means comparing extracted identifiers or claims in a document against a trusted external or curated reference source. A non-match should trigger review, while an unavailable registry should be disclosed as a coverage limitation.
