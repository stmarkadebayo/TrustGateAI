# Nigeria Vendor Review Documents

For Nigerian vendor and contractor review, teams commonly expect a packet that can support business identity, tax posture, qualifications, and the reason the vendor is eligible for the work.

Common review items include CAC or related registration support, tax clearance style documents, qualification or registration evidence, OEM authorization where relevant, and the commercial documents that connect the submission to an actual procurement or service request.

A reviewer should not treat a document bundle as complete merely because one strong document is present. The bundle should be assessed for completeness, consistency of business identity, consistency of dates, and consistency of referenced commercial records.
