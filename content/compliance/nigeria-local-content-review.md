# Nigeria Local Content Review

In Nigerian oil and gas compliance work, a local content review often asks whether the contractor or vendor has supplied enough evidence to support local participation claims, local sourcing claims, and qualification to perform the work.

Reviewers should look for consistency between the commercial transaction documents and the supporting compliance documents. If the vendor name, registration identifiers, or supporting authorization documents do not align, the submission should be escalated for manual review.

Where local content or qualification support is expected but not present, the review output should say that the coverage is incomplete instead of treating the absence as neutral.
