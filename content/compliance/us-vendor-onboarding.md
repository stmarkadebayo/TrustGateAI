# United States Vendor Onboarding

For United States vendor onboarding, teams often look for a practical support packet that helps establish who the vendor is, how they should be treated in procurement or finance systems, and whether the support documents are internally consistent.

Common review items can include business registration support, a W-9 or tax identity support document, certificates of insurance where required, and any authorization or qualification letters relevant to the engagement.

If the business identity shown across the packet is inconsistent, or if a tax identity support document is missing where it should normally exist, the onboarding review should be flagged for manual follow-up.
