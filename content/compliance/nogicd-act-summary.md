# NOGICD Act Summary

The Nigerian Oil and Gas Industry Content Development Act promotes Nigerian participation in oil and gas projects through local capability development, procurement preference, and documentation of local content performance.

Operators and contractors should maintain evidence showing how Nigerian goods, services, personnel, and facilities were prioritized where commercially and technically feasible.

Compliance reviews typically consider whether procurement decisions, subcontracting structures, and workforce composition align with approved local content plans and statutory expectations.
