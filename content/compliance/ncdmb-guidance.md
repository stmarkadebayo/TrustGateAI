# NCDMB Local Content Guidance

NCDMB guidance expects contractors and operators to keep clear supporting records for vendor qualification, local participation claims, training commitments, and sourcing decisions.

A credible compliance pack usually includes registration details, approvals, invoices, contractual references, and proof that local options were evaluated before offshore or foreign alternatives were selected.

When documentation is incomplete, inconsistent, or unsupported by referenced records, the issue should be escalated for manual compliance review rather than treated as compliant by default.
